Habitat:

Open skies, digital atmospheres, and interactive canvas environments. It prefers bright blue backgrounds and areas where it can drift freely!!!

Appearance:

A soft white floating cloud with round fluffy edges, small black eyes, and a simple curved mouth. It gently wiggles and moves across the sky.

Key Characteristics:

  • Flies in the sky.
  • Gets angry when too big.
  • Splits when size too big.
  • Enjoy someone helps it destress

What we know so far about Cloud(ies)? Cloud(ies) is a responsive sky creature that reacts to touch. It can grow, rain, and even split into multiple smaller versions of itself when it drifts beyond its boundaries. Its behavior is controlled by motion patterns and user interaction.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Press and hold on Cloud(ies) to make it rain. Release to calm it. If it floats beyond the frame, it will split into smaller clouds. Interaction changes its size and behavior in real time.
